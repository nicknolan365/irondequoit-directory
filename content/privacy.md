## Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. This notice explains our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. It is available on our homepage and at every point where personally identifiable information may be requested.

## Collection of Personal Information

When visiting irondequoitdirectory.com, the IP address used to access the site will be logged along with the dates and times of access. This information is used solely to analyze trends, administer the site, track user movement, and gather broad demographic information for internal use. Recorded IP addresses are not linked to personally identifiable information.

## Email Collection

When you sign up to receive a guide or join our email list, we collect your name and email address. We use this information only to send you the requested content and occasional emails with updates, tips, and promotions related to Irondequoit Directory. We do not sell, share, or rent your email address to third parties. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link included in any email.

## Links to Third-Party Websites

This site includes links to other websites for your use and reference. We are not responsible for the privacy policies of those websites. The privacy practices of third-party sites may differ from our own, and we encourage you to review their policies.

## Changes to This Privacy Statement

The contents of this statement may be updated at any time at our discretion. We recommend checking this page periodically for any changes.

## Questions

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or the website, contact us at [hello@irondequoitdirectory.com](mailto:hello@irondequoitdirectory.com).

## Accessibility

Irondequoit Directory is committed to making our website usable for everyone. We aim for irondequoitdirectory.com and its main pages to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA.

### What We Do

We use semantic HTML, headings, labels, and alt text. We maintain sufficient color contrast and visible focus states. We support full keyboard navigation and logical tab order. We provide text alternatives for images and media and avoid autoplay. We test new pages with automated and manual accessibility checks.

### If You Need Help or an Alternative Format

If you have trouble using our site or need information in a different format, contact us and we'll help.

**Email:** [hello@irondequoitdirectory.com](mailto:hello@irondequoitdirectory.com)

### Feedback and Response Time

Tell us about any accessibility barriers you encounter. We try to respond within two business days.

### Compatibility

This site is designed to work with current versions of major browsers and assistive technologies.

### Third-Party Content

Some pages may include third-party tools or embedded content that we don't fully control. We'll do our best to provide accessible alternatives when needed.

### Ongoing Improvement

We review our site regularly and update this section as we improve.
